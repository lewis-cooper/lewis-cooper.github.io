01 PhD Research

Supermassive Black Hole Binaries

My research sits at the intersection of observational astronomy and computational methods. I study the environments and dynamics of active galactic nuclei (AGN) — the extremely luminous centres of galaxies powered by accreting supermassive black holes — with a particular focus on binary quasar systems and what they reveal about galaxy mergers and black hole co-evolution.

Methods & Tools

  • Spectroscopic analysis
  • Statistical modelling
  • Python
  • Machine learning
